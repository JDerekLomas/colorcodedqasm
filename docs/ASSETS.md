## MuleRouter Prompt Templates

Use these prompts with MuleRouter to generate similar assets:

### Product Photography
```
Professional product photography of [GARMENT TYPE] with [EMBROIDERY/PATTERN STYLE] embroidery,
shot on [BACKGROUND COLOR] background, soft natural lighting, high-end fashion e-commerce style,
detailed stitching visible, [COLOR PALETTE], 4:5 aspect ratio
```

### Portrait Photography
```
Creative professional portrait of [DESCRIPTION] artisan/designer,
warm natural lighting, shallow depth of field, creative workspace setting,
authentic and approachable expression, editorial style, square crop
```

### Motif Illustrations
```
[SUBJECT] illustration in traditional South Asian art style,
decorative and elegant, [COLOR PALETTE], suitable for textile embroidery,
detailed linework, botanical/natural elements, transparent background PNG
```

### Fabric Texture Swatches
```
Close-up macro photography of [FABRIC TYPE] fabric texture,
showing weave pattern and material quality, natural lighting,
[COLOR] color palette, square crop for carousel display
```

### Video Generation (for tools that support it)
```
Cinematic footage of artisan hands [ACTION: weaving/embroidering/spinning],
warm workshop lighting, shallow depth of field,
slow motion detail shots, loopable, [DURATION] seconds
```
